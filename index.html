About us

Welcome to the official website of the City of Dunfermline!

 

Dunfermline, located in Fife, Scotland, is a historic town with a rich heritage dating back to medieval times. As the birthplace of Andrew Carnegie, Dunfermline has a strong connection to industry and innovation.

 

Our website aims to provide residents and visitors with valuable information about local events, attractions, services, and community resources. Whether you are looking to explore our historic sites, enjoy our beautiful parks, or learn about our vibrant arts and culture scene, the City of Dunfermline has something for everyone.

 

We invite you to browse our website and discover all that our city has to offer. Welcome to Dunfermline!
